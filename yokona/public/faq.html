Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Yokona?
A: Yokona is a Filipino-English forum for posting, overposting, and occasionally, class struggle. We discuss everything from theory debates to full-blown meme psychosis.
Q: What are the rules?
A: No MAGAtards, anarcho-furries, or Facebook centrists. Post theory or post hole. NSFW allowed but no crime or cringe. Touch grass, not your cousin. Long live Supreme Leader Kuya Ambo.
Q: Do I need to register an account?
A: No! Yokona is completely anonymous. Just post and enjoy the dialectical chaos.
Q: What does "Post theory or post hole" mean?
A: Either contribute meaningful theoretical discussion or don't post at all. We don't tolerate low-effort posts.
Q: Can I post images?
A: Yes! Images are welcome in both threads and replies. Keep them relevant and within site rules.

A: Contact the moderators through the contact page. However, remember: mods are Godless, don't beg.
