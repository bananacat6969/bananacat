Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What the fuck is Yokona?
A: A textboard for the overread and the overstimulated. Tagalog-English mix, where schizo memes meet schizo theory. If you’re here for clean UX and normal opinions, leave.
Q: Are there rules or can I just post my balls?
A: You can post your balls but no gore, no CP, no unsolicited nipples, No MAGAtards, anarcho-furries, or Facebook centrists. Memes, screenshots, propaganda? Feed us.
Q: Is this leftist Reddit?
A: If Reddit had any testicles, they would've been vaporized by now. Yokona isn't leftist™️, it’s leftist feral. You'll find Marxist-Leninists, anarchists, tankies, vegans, vapers, and people who think Stalin was too soft.
Q: Do I need to register an account?
A: No! Yokona is completely anonymous. Just post and enjoy the dialectical chaos.
Q: What does "Post theory or post hole" mean?
A: Either contribute meaningful theoretical discussion or don't post at all. We don't tolerate low-effort posts.
Q: Can I post images?
A: Yes! Images are welcome in both threads and replies. Keep them relevant and within site rules.
Q: What is a "coño post"?
A: Privileged, out-of-touch posts that show disconnect from working-class struggles. These get you cyber-gulagged.
Q: Who is Ka Silang?
A: The unholy trinity: site admin, coder, theorist. Probably arguing with five people while pushing a Git update.
Q: How do I report problematic content?
A: Contact the moderators through the contact page. See something illegal, cringe, or CIA-coded? Report it. Also, reporting dumb shit might get you banned out of spite.
Q: Will this site always be free?
A: Enjoy it while it's free. But if it gets overrun by crypto bros and wannabe influencers, Ka Silang might ban the internet entirely.
